Why we are trying to make the world more resourceful

Our story

At Founders of Tomorrow 2019 a group of passionate individuals got united to find solutions to prevent a further global warming of 1.5 degrees. A group of 5 diverse skilled professionals decided to work on SDG 11 on sustainable cities and communities.

We all knew waste was an issue in cities, but when we discovered that a third of the worlds waste comes from construction even though 90% of buildings can be reused, we were flabbergasted and decided we needed to close this gap.

4 months, hundreds of talks with industry experts & hundreds of research papers later, we decided we had to be the ones that onboarded the construction industry to the circular economy leveraging exponential technologies.

Impact where change is needed

All members of the founding team are changemakers willing to pursue impact over anything else. We agree we no longer want to attempt changing the industry by selling the sustainable circular dream through architecture agencies.

the industry will never become 100% sustainable under the current structure.

We know the industry want to make the switch, but lack the technological innovations to get there. That's why BeResourceful exist.

Sustainability through circularity

The circular economy has transformed many industries due to individuals being able to save money & make money by being more resourceful. This hasn't arrived to the construction industry yet, because it haven't been possible to trace the materials. To enable the circular economy is traceability key.

Vision

We believe in a modular future for the construction industry. A future where buildings can be taken apart and rebuild as needed.

No waste

When everything is designed for disassembly and sustainable materials are being used, no components will need to be destroyed and wasted, but can instead be reused for new construction and renovation.

Imagine

Imagine a future where buildings talk to us to optimize performance. Where the building owner can remove a building and set it up in a new place when needed. Where tenants can decide to add and subtract components as needed to their houses.

Inevitable

We are certain that in the future all building components will be intelligent. This change is both needed for the environment and all businesses will need to adopt to this change in order to stay resourceful and competitive.
We want to be the ones pushing this change to come faster than later, so we can prevent the world's temperature to increase the 1.5 degrees it is set to.
If you are interested in creating this future with us, get in touch.
